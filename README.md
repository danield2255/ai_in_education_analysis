# ai_in_education_analysis
An exploration of how AI is viewed and used in the education system. 


## Data
The data used is from a 16-question survey on the perception of AI in education at the Faculty of Cybernetics, Statistics, and Economic Informatics in Romania. 
It can be found on Kaggle at https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/gianinamariapetrascu/survey-on-students-perceptions-of-ai-in-education/data
